What are Fractions?

Fractions are part of your everyday life - most of the time you don't even realize that you are using them. Here are some examples you may have encountered:

Half a pizza.

A third of a cup.

A quarter dollar.

Definition

Fractions represent parts of a whole. Fractions have a numerator and a denominator. The numerator represents the number of equal parts and the denominator represents the total amount of parts that make up a whole. For example 2/5, the numerator is 2 and the denominator is 5. In half a pizza, the numerator is 1 and the denominator is 2 - that makes 1/2 a pizza!
